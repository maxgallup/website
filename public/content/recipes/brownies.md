# Brownies
11/03/2021

\

### Ingredients

* 1 cup flour
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 6 tablespoons cocoa
* 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 cup water
* 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
* 1/2 cup applesauce
* 1 teaspoon vanilla


### Procedure
Mix into a bowl and bake for 25 minutes at 175°C (347°F).
